Featured Programs

Discover our most popular learning adventures

STEAM Series

From bubbling lava lamps to engineering towers from marshmallows, our STEAM Series blends science, technology, engineering, art, and math in a creative, high-energy environment.

  • Hands-on labs
  • Reflection activities
  • Take-home challenges
Storybook Collection

Over six magical weeks, students explore what makes a story come to life—from setting and character to plot and point of view. Each child works toward publishing their own short story.

  • Character development
  • Plot structure
  • Published story
Lunar Explorers

Dive into the mysteries of the moon! This 8-week workshop explores moon phases, tides, legends, and lunar science through experiments, art projects, and a final showcase diorama.

  • Moon phases
  • Lunar science
  • Showcase diorama
